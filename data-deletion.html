Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Void Apps apps require no account and most data we use is anonymous and not tied to your identity. You can still request deletion of any data associated with you.

Delete data yourself (instant)

  • Uninstall the app — this removes all data stored on your device by the app.
  • Reset your Advertising ID in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads, to disconnect advertising data.

Request deletion from us

Email cevat.akdmrr@gmail.com with the subject “Data deletion request” and include:

  • The app name
  • Your Advertising ID and/or RevenueCat app user ID, if available (usually shown in the app’s Settings → About)

We will forward the request to our processors (Google AdMob, PostHog, RevenueCat) and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Anonymous analytics events associated with your identifiers (PostHog)
  • Advertising data associated with your Advertising ID (where Google allows)
  • Subscription records tied to your anonymous app user ID (RevenueCat), subject to legal/billing retention requirements

Note: purchase records required for tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention may be retained as required by law even after a deletion request.
